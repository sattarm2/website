About Us

We offer a range of luxurious cars which you can hire at low prices for all sorts of different occasions. We are based in Leicester and we lend our cars out all over the UK. We are very reliable and have made a lot of happy faces! We specialise in the high end cars such as Lamborghini, Ferrari, Porsh and many more. Although we are based in Leicester we have customers all over the UK and we strive to provide you with the best service possible. We have a reasonable quantity of each of our cars so we can provide customers with cars more or less all the time. Our services are available Monday to Saturday so feel free to give us a call or send us an email for any queries. Our staff are well trained and have been taught to offer their services in a highly presentable way as we belive service is an important technique in business. Our team at Cars 4 Hire are able to cater for any car rental requirement you might need throughout the UK.
